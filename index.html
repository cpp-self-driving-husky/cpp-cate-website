What is CATE?

The Cal Poly Autonomous Transportation Experience is a student and faculty guided project for developing self-driving vehicles that will be used on the Cal Poly Pomona campus.

Our Vision

Imagine you invite someone for a tour of the Cal Poly Pomona campus. It might be your friend who is interested in starting here, it might be a potential investor, or it might just be your parents. They come here for the tour and are greeted by a guide at the parking lot with a designated tour vehicle; as everybody sits down, the guide pushes a few buttons on a tablet. The vehicle then starts driving by itself; the guide can easily face the visitors, describe the beautiful campus, as well as share information about the vehicle they are currently sitting in, an autonomous vehicle developed by students from multiple disciplines.
