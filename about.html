Why?

To bring students from multiple disciplines together to foster creativity and problem solving skills to implement solutions to the real world problems of self-driving cars. This in term can also be used to attract investors and new students.

What?

By forming multidisciplinary teams to provide solutions to services that when combined will enable the vehicle to be commanded to drive to a specific location or along a specific route safely in a timely manner. These services include but is not limited to tasks such as obstacle detection, obstacle avoidance, path planning, sensor fusion, and speed and heading control.
